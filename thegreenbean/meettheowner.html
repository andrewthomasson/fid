MEET JEFF

Jeff Smith, founder of The Green Bean, is a 35-year-old coffee enthusiast with a heart for hospitality and a deep-rooted love for his community. With over a decade of experience in the food and beverage industry, Jeff took a leap of faith five years ago by launching The Green Bean from a refurbished food truck. He believed that coffee could do more than just wake people up—it could bring them together. That vision quickly gained traction, turning a street-side operation into a local favorite, and eventually into a welcoming brick-and-mortar café known for its warm atmosphere and thoughtfully crafted drinks.

Beyond the café, Jeff is a devoted husband and proud father of two, balancing early morning roasts and business strategy with school pickups and family dinners. He’s known for remembering customers’ names and orders, jumping behind the counter during busy hours, and supporting local artists, bakers, and fellow small business owners. Jeff’s commitment to quality and community is the heartbeat of The Green Bean, and his approachable, hands-on leadership style has helped turn a small coffee venture into a beloved neighborhood staple.
