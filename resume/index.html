About Andrew

Welcome!
Andrew Thomasson is an artist, illustrator, and graphic designer with a lifetime of art and design experience and over a decade of leadership, teaching, and communication experience.

In a world that is so often lacking in beauty in many ways, Andrew is passionate about uncovering the beauty that exists all around us. As a designer, he enjoys adding an aesthetically pleasing touch to other people's products. These could include branding and logos for businesses as well as creating designs for various forms of merch. He is also passionate about bringing words and concepts to life through art and illustration.

Andrew is a Northeast Florida native and graduated from the University of North Florida in 2009. He has lived in Jacksonville, Fl; Raleigh, NC; and Seattle, WA. His education includes a BA in Psychology with a minor in Studio Art, a Master of Divinity where he began studying the theology and philosophy of beauty and aesthetics, and an AS in Graphic Design.
